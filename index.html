About Me

Full-Stack Developer & Research Enthusiast

Hello! I'm Aayush Swami, a Computer Science student at Arizona State University, passionate about software engineering, real-time systems, and emerging technologies. I enjoy turning complex challenges into elegant technical solutions that create real-world impact.

My journey began in high school when I first discovered coding, and since then, I've deepened my skills through academic research, internships, and personal projects. I specialize in the MERN stack, Next.js, TypeScript, Python, and Java, and have worked on applications ranging from mission-critical dashboards to intelligent data systems.

Some highlights from my recent work include:

  • Developing a real-time rover dashboard with Socket.io, InfluxDB, and OpenAI for ASU’s Interplanetary Lab
  • Building a machine learning pipeline to predict lithium-ion battery health using GPR in Python at Rolston Lab
  • Creating an automated LEGO car controlled via Java and MATLAB
  • Contributing to NASA’s Space App Challenge with a data-driven website built around solar activity

Beyond technical work, I serve as a Student Engagement Peer Coordinator at ASU, where I help manage 50+ student organizations and oversee $250,000+ in annual funding. I also represent the university as a Fulton Ambassador and am proud to be an Adobe Student Ambassador.

I'm always open to learning, growing, and collaborating on impactful projects. Let’s connect and build something awesome together!
